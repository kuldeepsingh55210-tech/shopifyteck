Everything you need to know

Find answers to common questions about platform setup, multilingual support, and operations.

How is ORYQX different from traditional tools? +
Traditional solutions charge base pricing on ticket volume, meaning you are penalized as your customer base scales. ORYQX charges a flat flat-rate model ($99/mo starting price) and provides a fully context-aware AI Memory Engine that automatically looks up order statuses and returns parameters to resolve 57%+ of queries without human agent logins.
Does it really work for multilingual customers? +
Yes. ORYQX natively reads and responds in over 85 languages. If a customer writes in Spanish, French, or Japanese, the AI reviews your English store policies, runs database order queries, and writes back a fully contextual reply in their native language within 1.2 seconds.
How long does setup take? +
Less than 5 minutes. Installing the app via Shopify is a one-click process. Once connected, upload or paste your shop's return parameters, shipping conditions, and FAQs, and the AI is ready to resolve customer support issues immediately.
What happens when AI can't resolve a ticket? +
When a query falls below your set confidence score or requires complex manual review, the ticket is instantly flagged and placed into the priority escalation queue inside your Shopify dashboard, notifying your support agents immediately.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. There are no lock-in terms or multi-year contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your ORYQX subscription directly through your Shopify merchant account admin at any time.
Is my customer data secure? +
Yes, data security is our top priority. ORYQX utilizes enterprise-grade encryption for all network transmissions and is SOC2 compliant. Customer profiles, order histories, and payment fields are tokenized securely and are never exposed publicly.
Do you offer custom enterprise plans? +
Yes. For high-volume storefronts processing more than 10,000 tickets monthly, we offer enterprise plans featuring dedicated support accounts, fine-tuned custom voice models, and tailored SLA performance guarantees.
What Shopify plans does ORYQX support? +
ORYQX supports all standard Shopify setups, including Basic Shopify, Shopify, Shopify Advanced, and Shopify Plus configurations.
